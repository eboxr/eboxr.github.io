Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a card?

Tap the + button on the main screen. You can scan a barcode with your camera or enter the card details manually.

How do I add a card to Apple Wallet?

Open a card and tap "Add to Apple Wallet". Your card's barcode, color, and icon will be included in the pass.

Is my data secure?

Yes. Card numbers are encrypted on your device with AES-256 before being stored. We also support two-factor authentication for your account.

What is the Pro upgrade?

The free version supports up to 5 cards. The Pro upgrade is a one-time purchase that unlocks unlimited cards — no subscriptions or recurring fees.

How do I delete my account?

Go to your Profile and tap "Delete Account". This permanently removes all your data from our servers.
